About Me

I am Malala Yousafzai, an education activist from Pakistan and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and create positive change in the world.

Since surviving an attack by the Taliban in 2012, I have become a global advocate for girls' education and women's rights. Through the Malala Fund, we work to ensure every girl has access to 12 years of free, safe, quality education.
